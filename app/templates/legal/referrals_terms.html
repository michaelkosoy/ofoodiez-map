Ofoodiez Referrals Bot — Terms of Service

Last updated: July 2026

The Ofoodiez Referrals Bot (the "Service") connects job seekers ("candidates") with employees at tech companies ("advocates") who may choose to submit an internal referral on the candidate's behalf. The Service is operated by Ofoodiez and runs over WhatsApp.

Your agreement

By registering with the bot, sending it your details or CV, or tapping "I agree" in the WhatsApp conversation, you accept these terms and our Privacy Policy. If you don't agree, please don't use the Service.

1. What the Service is (and isn't)

  • The Service is free for candidates.
  • Advocates participate voluntarily. Ofoodiez is not a recruitment agency, does not represent the companies listed, and receives no instruction from them.
  • Company names appearing in the Service belong to their respective owners and do not imply any affiliation with or endorsement of Ofoodiez.

2. No guarantees

We do our best to route your CV to a real advocate, but we cannot and do not promise:

  • that an advocate will submit your referral,
  • that you will get an interview, a response, or a job,
  • any timeline for a referral being handled.

A confirmed referral means an advocate told us they submitted it — the hiring process itself belongs entirely to the company.

3. Your information

To operate the Service we collect your name, WhatsApp number, email address, your CV, and the companies and roles you ask about. This information — including your CV — is kept in our database so we can process your requests and follow up on them.

  • When you request a referral, your name, email and CV are shared with the advocate(s) at the company you chose — that's the point of the Service.
  • We keep your details and CV on file so we can offer you other relevant job opportunities that match your profile. If you'd rather we didn't, ask us to remove your data at any time.
  • Advocates' identities are shared with candidates only if the advocate chose to share their contact details.
  • We never sell your data. See the Privacy Policy for details, and the data deletion page to have your data removed.

4. Your responsibilities

  • Provide accurate, truthful information and only your own CV.
  • Use the Service for genuine job searching — no spam, scraping, or bulk requests.
  • You must be at least 18 years old.

5. Advocates

  • Advocates confirm only referrals they actually submitted, and may decline or ignore any request at their sole discretion.
  • Advocates are responsible for complying with their employer's referral policies.
  • Advocates can leave the program at any time by messaging the bot.
