Privacy Policy

How oFoodiez Instagram Automation handles your data

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Introduction

oFoodiez Instagram Automation ("the Service") is operated by oFoodiez. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Instagram DM and comment automation service.

2. Data We Collect

When you connect your Instagram account, we collect and store:

  • Account Information: Your Instagram User ID, username, account type, and profile picture URL.
  • Access Tokens: OAuth access tokens provided by Meta to interact with the Instagram Graph API on your behalf.
  • Message Data: Incoming and outgoing messages processed through automations, including text content, timestamps, and message IDs.
  • Comment Data: Comments on your posts that trigger automations, including the commenter's username and comment text.
  • Contact Information: Instagram-Scoped IDs (IGSIDs) and usernames of people who interact with your account through the Service.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data exclusively to:

  • Process and execute your configured automations (comment-to-DM, keyword triggers, etc.)
  • Display your inbox, contacts, and automation statistics in the dashboard
  • Maintain message logs for your review
  • Refresh your access tokens to maintain service continuity

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only transmitted to Meta's Instagram Graph API to fulfill the actions you have configured (e.g., sending automated DMs in response to comments).

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored in a secure database hosted on Render's infrastructure.
  • Access tokens are stored securely and are only used for authorized API calls.
  • All communication with Meta's APIs is done over HTTPS.
  • We employ industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your Instagram account is connected to the Service. When you disconnect your account, we deactivate your user record. You may request full deletion of your data at any time (see section 8).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the dashboard
  • Disconnect your Instagram account at any time
  • Delete all your data by submitting a data deletion request
  • Revoke API access via your Instagram account settings

8. Data Deletion

To request deletion of all your data, visit our Data Deletion page or email us at privacy@ofoodiez.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

9. Meta Platform Terms

This Service uses Meta's Instagram Graph API. By using the Service, you also agree to Meta's Terms of Service and Meta Platform Terms.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
privacy@ofoodiez.com
